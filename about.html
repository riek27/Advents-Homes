About Calem Custom Homes

20 years of excellence in custom home building and remodeling

Our Story

Calem Custom Homes is one of many companies owned by Robert Traylor. With 20 years of experience in the industry, Robert has built a reputation for excellence through multiple successful ventures including Executive Basements, Executive Kitchen and Bath, Executive Exteriors, Executive Radon and Granite Specialties.

Our integrated approach allows us to be your one-stop-shop for any home improvement project. We pride ourselves on building lasting relationships with our homeowners, and by providing comprehensive home improvement services, we can handle any project from start to finish.

Our philosophy is simple: treat every home as if it were our own. This commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for custom homes in Colorado and beyond.

OUR PROCESS

A streamlined approach to building your dream home

1. Architect Planning

Introductory meeting with Calem Custom Homes to assemble your wish list. Design the home of your dreams and look at potential home sites.

2. Design Development

Meet with Jon Rentfrow, our exclusive architect, to design your home. Finalize your home plans, finalize your home site, finalize the cost of your home.

3. Contract & Permits

Contractual agreements and permit application process. We handle all the paperwork so you can focus on the exciting parts.

4. Construction

Let the building begin! Our team of skilled professionals brings your dream home to life with precision and care.

WHY IS CALEM DIFFERENT?

Quality Over Quantity

Unlike most home builders whose goals are to produce as many homes as they can and focus merely on the numbers, we build each home as if it's our own. Our primary goal is perfection and quality.

Expert Team

Any home builder is only as good as it's sub contractors but here at Calem our team of seasoned professionals share the same drive and passion for high quality finishes that is reflected in the finished product.

Relationship Focused

During our 20 years as a general contractor/home builder who specializes in high end custom homes and remodeling services, Calem believes the most important aspect of the business is it's relationships with its clients. It's this belief that has 90% of all clients and potential projects are referred business.

Value & Transparency

Calem Custom Homes and its companies purchase the majority of its building materials directly from the source saving you money on the high quality items that make your home or project special. Couple that with a philosophy based on high ethics and transparency you can believe you will get the best bang for your buck with Calem Custom Homes.
