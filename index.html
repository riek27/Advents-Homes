WHY IS CALEM DIFFERENT?

Unlike most home builders whose goals are to produce as many homes as they can and focus merely on the numbers, we build each home as if it's our own. Our primary goal is perfection and quality.

Any home builder is only as good as it's sub contractors but here at Calem our team of seasoned professionals share the same drive and passion for high quality finishes that is reflected in the finished product.

During our 20 years as a general contractor/home builder who specializes in high end custom homes and remodeling services, Calem believes the most important aspect of the business is it's relationships with its clients. It's this belief that has 90% of all clients and potential projects are referred business.

Calem Custom Homes and its companies purchase the majority of its building materials directly from the source saving you money on the high quality items that make your home or project special. Couple that with a philosophy based on high ethics and transparency you can believe you will get the best bang for your buck with Calem Custom Homes.
