Our Services

Comprehensive home improvement solutions for every need

WHAT WE DO

Your complete home improvement partner with over 20 years of excellence

Calem Custom Homes is your one-stop-shop for any home improvement project. As part of the Robert Traylor family of companies, we offer comprehensive services through our specialized divisions, ensuring expert attention to every aspect of your project.

Custom Home Construction

We specialize in creating completely custom residences from the ground up. Our process begins with extensive consultation to understand your lifestyle, preferences, and vision. From architectural design to the final finishes, every detail is meticulously planned and executed to create a home that is uniquely yours.

Our custom homes feature premium materials, innovative technology integration, and sustainable building practices that exceed industry standards. We work with our exclusive architect, Jon Rentfrow, to ensure your home is both beautiful and functional.

Executive Basements

Transform your unfinished basement into valuable living space with Executive Basements. We create functional, beautiful basements that add significant value to your home. From home theaters and game rooms to additional bedrooms and living areas, we design basements that meet your family's needs.

Our basement finishing services include waterproofing, insulation, electrical work, plumbing, drywall, flooring, and custom finishes. We handle all aspects of the project to ensure a seamless transformation.

Executive Kitchen & Bath

Create the kitchen or bathroom of your dreams with Executive Kitchen & Bath. We specialize in luxury kitchen and bathroom remodeling that combines functionality with stunning design. Our team works with you to create spaces that reflect your personal style while meeting your practical needs.

Services include custom cabinetry, countertop installation, plumbing and electrical work, lighting design, tile work, and premium appliance installation. We source materials directly from manufacturers to provide the best value.

Granite Specialties

Elevate your home with premium granite countertops and stone surfaces from Granite Specialties. We offer a wide selection of granite, marble, quartz, and other natural stones for kitchens, bathrooms, and other applications.

Our services include custom fabrication, precise installation, sealing, and maintenance. We work with you to select the perfect stone that complements your design aesthetic and lifestyle needs.

Executive Exteriors

Enhance your home's curb appeal and protection with Executive Exteriors. We provide comprehensive exterior services including siding, roofing, windows, doors, and outdoor living spaces.

Our exterior solutions are designed to withstand Colorado's climate while enhancing your home's beauty and value. We use high-quality materials and expert installation techniques to ensure lasting results.
